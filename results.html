Repoire Network

The network is shown as a force-based network layout where messages are like "charges" that repel each other and links between messages act like "springs" pulling them together with a restoring force.

  • Hovering over a submission shows the filename, semantic size, and links to other submissions.
  • Clicking on a submission will bring up another window where you can try a quantitative match to that submission
  • There is also a search form below were you can search for a submission by its filename: matching results will be blackened in the plot.
